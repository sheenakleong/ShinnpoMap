The Mission of Shinnpo

According to numerous studies on child development, learning languages at a young age stimulates children's brains by providing educational challenges that lead to being able to retain information easier, better memorization, develop open-mindedness, and generate curiosity. Shinnpo - meaning "advance" in Japanese - advocates this by providing a child-friendly (and adult!) adventure-style game that lets you learn basic communication around the world.
